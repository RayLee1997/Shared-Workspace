## Summary

The Apple Vision Pro is finally getting an official visionOS YouTube app on Thursday. With the visionOS YouTube app, you'll be able to watch all videos available on YouTube, including standard videos, Shorts, 3D, 360, and VR180 videos, Apple spokesperson Corey Nord tells The Verge.
The Vision Pro launched more than two years ago, but it hasn't had an official YouTube app until now. Ahead of the release of the Vision Pro, YouTube said that it wasn't planning to build a native app for the platform.
